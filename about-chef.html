🥩About Chef's Favourite

About the New Version App
Chef's Favourite is a modern, private recipe and kitchen wisdom platform built for home cooks and food lovers.

The app lets you save, organize, and search your favorite recipes and tips, all stored securely in your browser.

Designed with accessibility, mobile support, and a beautiful green-themed interface, Chef's Favourite makes cooking and learning easy and enjoyable for everyone.

Fun fact: Chef's Favourite was inspired by the idea that every cook deserves a personal, private cookbook—no account needed!
Chef's Favourite is a private recipe platform designed for home cooks and food lovers.

Discover new dishes, save your own creations, and explore helpful kitchen wisdom—all in your own personal space.

What's new? The latest version brings a full-page responsive layout, animated entries, mobile menu toggle, dark mode, accessibility improvements, progress bars, green-themed design, and a fancy back button.

Every feature is made for your convenience: add recipes, organize tips, and build your own digital cookbook. Your experience is unique and tailored just for you. It is also accessible on mobile devices.

Want to keep track of your favorite recipes or kitchen tricks? Visit the Recipe Book or Tips & Tricks page and start building your private collection!

FAQ
Is my data private?
Yes! All recipes and tips are stored only in your browser and are never shared.
Can I use Chef's Favourite on my phone?
Absolutely. The site is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices.
How do I add my own tips?
Go to the Tips & Tricks page and use the "Add Your Own" feature.
How do I enable dark mode?
Click the moon icon at the bottom right of the page to toggle dark mode on or off.
What is the real name of the person who made Chef's Favourite?
Chef's Favourite was created by Doreen Baker.
